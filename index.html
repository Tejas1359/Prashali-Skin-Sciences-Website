Hair Loss Treatment in Nelamangala & Bangalore

Hair fall affects millions of men and women across Bangalore — triggered by stress, nutritional deficiencies, hormonal changes, and genetics. At Prashali Skin Sciences, Dr. Varsha R Koti diagnoses the exact cause of your hair loss and creates a personalised plan to stop the fall and stimulate regrowth.

Why Is My Hair Falling Out?

The most common causes in Bangalore include androgenetic alopecia (genetic hair loss), telogen effluvium (sudden hair fall after stress, illness, or post-pregnancy), nutritional deficiencies (iron, vitamin D, B12), scalp conditions (dandruff, seborrhoeic dermatitis), and thyroid imbalance. A proper diagnosis is essential before starting treatment — the wrong treatment can make hair loss worse.

Hair Loss Treatments Available

  • PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy – proven to reduce fall and stimulate regrowth
  • GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) therapy – advanced, highly concentrated alternative to PRP
  • Mesotherapy – vitamin and growth factor injections directly to the scalp
  • Medical management: minoxidil, finasteride, topical treatments
  • Nutritional assessment and supplementation guidance

FAQs – Hair Loss Treatment

Is PRP hair treatment painful?
PRP is mildly uncomfortable but not painful. A numbing cream is applied before the procedure to ensure you're comfortable throughout.
How many PRP sessions do I need to see results?
Most patients see reduced hair fall after 3–4 sessions. A full course is typically 6 sessions over 6 months, followed by quarterly maintenance.
Can women also take PRP treatment?
Yes. PRP is highly effective for female pattern hair loss as well as post-pregnancy hair fall. Dr. Varsha treats both men and women with hair loss.
What is the cost of PRP hair treatment in Nelamangala?
PRP hair treatment starts from ₹3,500 per session at our Nelamangala clinic. Packages for full treatment courses are available at discounted rates.

Pigmentation Treatment in Bangalore

Pigmentation — whether melasma, post-acne marks, sun tan, or age spots — is one of the top skin concerns for women in Bangalore. At Prashali Skin Sciences, we offer proven, safe pigmentation treatment designed specifically for Indian skin.

Types of Pigmentation We Treat

  • Melasma (pregnancy mask, hormonal pigmentation)
  • Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH) from acne or injuries
  • Sun tan and UV-induced dark spots
  • Freckles and age spots
  • Perioral pigmentation (dark patches around the mouth)
  • Periorbital pigmentation (dark circles)

Why Pigmentation Treatment Requires a Specialist

Indian skin is especially prone to post-treatment hyperpigmentation if treated aggressively. Generic peels or over-the-counter lightening creams can worsen pigmentation in darker skin tones. Dr. Varsha's approach is conservative, safe, and calibrated specifically to your Fitzpatrick skin type.

Pigmentation Treatment FAQs

Can melasma be permanently cured?
Melasma is a chronic condition influenced by sun exposure and hormones. Treatment can dramatically reduce it and keep it controlled, but strict sun protection must be maintained lifelong to prevent recurrence.
How many sessions of laser treatment are needed for pigmentation?
Most patients need 4–6 laser sessions spaced 3–4 weeks apart for optimal results. This is combined with a prescribed home-care regimen.
What is the cost of pigmentation treatment in Bangalore?
Chemical peels start from ₹600/session. Q-switch laser starts from ₹1,500/session. The number of sessions required depends on the type and severity of pigmentation.
